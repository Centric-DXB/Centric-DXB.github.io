Who we are

Al Moammar Information Systems Co. (MIS) is one of the largest IT companies in KSA established in 1979, and the first Saudi public listed company, which provides integrated solutions for the information systems with outstanding quality and suitable technical products for the international standards and specifications.

Our partnerships are global to ensure the provision of technical services and the best infrastructure systems in line with the Vision 2030 of Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

  • Chairman Message
  • Vice Chairman Message
  • CEO Message

Khaled AL-Moammar

Chairman

The company has recognized the importance of digital business amid the pandemic and shifted its focus towards developing advanced digital capabilities to support the Kingdom's economic recovery and resilience. The Board of Directors has designated 2022 as a year of growth and innovation, transitioning from being a technology solutions provider to becoming a developer of smart digital projects and platforms aligned with Saudi Vision 2030. To achieve this goal, an Investment and Business Development Committee has been established to identify the best investment opportunities.

The Digital Bank has been licensed to lead the financial services sector, while health services are being invested in, supported by Excellence Medical Systems. "MIS Forward" has been established to generate high revenues through digital platforms and initiatives, and the company has partnered with Abunayyan Holding to build green data centers powered by renewable energy. These initiatives will significantly impact the company's market position and generate higher profits for its investors, while elevating the national digital system to match that of the most advanced countries in the world.

Khaled AL-Moammar

Vice Chairman

As Vice Chairman, I am pleased to share that in 2023, our company will prioritize the development of Saudi digital and smart projects, platforms, and capacities. The Investment and Business Development Committee has been formed to identify the best business and investment opportunities. We have received a license for our Digital Bank and will play a significant role in providing top-quality health services in the Kingdom. We have established "MIS Forward" to launch startups and have developed several digital platforms and initiatives. We have partnered with Abunayyan Holding to build green data centers and established the Saudi Data Center Fund 1. We pledge to continue developing groundbreaking projects and ideas to upgrade the national digital system.

Dr. Abdullah Al Ghamdi

Chief Executive Officer

Dr. Abdullah AlGhamdi is currently the CEO of MIS and MIS Forward Co. He was previously former Acting Director General of the National Information Center (one of the largest Data Center and IT Solutions/Services Provider at its time) and was Assistance Director General for Technical Affairs.

Vision

IT Solution Provider

To be the most distinguished IT solutions and services provider to the Kingdom and the region. It encompasses a wide range of technologies, including hardware, software, telecommunications, and services.

Mision

Revolutionizing MIS

Empower industries with technology solutions that matter, Inspiring vibrant societies in a world embracing intelligence and accessibility. The Information Technology (IT) industry is a sector that deals with the development, design, implementation, support, and management of computer-based information systems.
